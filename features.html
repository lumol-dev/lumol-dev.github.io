Frequently Asked Questions

Setup & Installation

How long does initial setup take?

Initial WiFi setup typically takes about 5 minutes. Simply connect to your WiFi network through our online configuration portal.

What happens if I enter the wrong WiFi password?

If an incorrect WiFi password is entered during setup, the device will automatically reboot after 1 minute of no network connection and return to setup mode.

Usage & Sync

What happens if WiFi connection is lost?

LUMOL will continue to display cached calendar data when WiFi is unavailable. Once connection is restored, it will automatically sync the latest updates.

Can I display multiple Google calendars?

Yes! LUMOL supports displaying multiple Google Calendar accounts simultaneously, allowing you to see all your events in one place.

Troubleshooting

How do I reset the device?

To reset LUMOL, disconnect it from WiFi and leave it disconnected for 5 minutes. This will restore factory settings.

What if the display becomes unresponsive?

If the display becomes unresponsive, simply restart the device. This typically resolves any temporary software issues.

My calendar isn't syncing properly. What should I do?

If sync fails, delete the device from your account on our website and re-add it. This will refresh the connection and resolve most sync issues.

How do I adjust timezone settings?

Timezone settings can be adjusted through the device manager on our website. LUMOL supports multiple timezones for travelers and remote workers.

What if gesture controls aren't working?

For hardware-related issues including gesture control problems, please contact our support team for assistance.

Updates & Support

How are firmware updates handled?

LUMOL receives automatic over-the-air updates. No manual intervention required - your device stays current with the latest features and security updates.

What kind of support is provided?

We provide lifetime support for all LUMOL devices, including software updates, troubleshooting assistance, and technical support.

How long do updates take to install?

Updates typically install within 2-3 minutes and happen automatically during low-usage periods to minimize disruption.

Can I contact support if I have issues?

Yes! Our support team is available 24/7 via email and chat. You can also access our comprehensive help documentation online.

Privacy & Security

What data does LUMOL collect?

LUMOL only accesses your calendar data necessary for display. We don't store or analyze your personal information beyond what's needed for functionality.

Is my calendar data secure?

Yes. All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption, and your calendar information is cached locally on the device only.

Can I revoke access at any time?

Absolutely. You can revoke LUMOL's access to your Google Calendar at any time through your Google account settings or our device manager.

Hardware & Compatibility

What WiFi networks does LUMOL support?

LUMOL supports standard 2.4GHz WiFi networks (802.11 b/g/n). It works with most home and office networks, including password-protected networks.

How much power does LUMOL consume?

LUMOL is extremely energy efficient, consuming only 0.5W of power - equivalent to leaving a small LED bulb on continuously.

Does LUMOL work with corporate WiFi networks?

LUMOL works with most standard corporate networks. For enterprise networks with special authentication, contact our support team for configuration assistance.

Will weather information be available in all locations?

Yes, when weather features are released, LUMOL will support weather information for locations worldwide using reliable weather data services.
